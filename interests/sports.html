Sports

Favourite Sport:

Hurling

My favourite sport is hurling. It is an original sport to this country and is played at a very fast pace. It offers great hours of entertainment during the summer watching matches both on the television and in person.

Teams I support:

Tipperary

The sports I have a major interest in hurling, football, soccer and rugby. I follow the Tipperary team in both hurling and football very closely. With soccer, I am keenly interested in Chelsea football club as I have supported them since a young age. Munster and Ireland are the two rugby teams I make a real effort to watch.

Sports played:

Hurling,Football,Rugby and Mountain biking

I personally am involved in hurling, football, rugby and also enjoy mountain biking. I am involved with these on a local level and greatly enjoy being in the fresh air and meeting others.
